Who Am I?

I’m a curious designer and developer who enjoys exploring the intersection of creativity and technology. I love turning ideas into interactive experiences.

What Sets Me Apart

What sets me apart is my ability to think visually and logically at the same time, crafting solutions that are both aesthetically engaging and practical.

Why This Program Fits Me

I chose this program because I realized my strength lies in creating visual and creative work. Before this program, I studied programming at PXL, but I discovered that this course suited me better. It gives me the freedom to combine technical skills with creativity and bring visual ideas to life.

What I Enjoy About This Program

I love creating dynamic user interfaces and exploring 3D visualization. Bringing an idea to life on screen is incredibly satisfying and motivates me to keep learning and experimenting.
